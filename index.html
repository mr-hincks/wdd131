Ghana, located in West Africa, is a country rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. It was the first sub-Saharan nation to gain independence from colonial rule in 1957, led by Kwame Nkrumah. Known for its vibrant traditions, Ghana is famous for kente cloth, lively festivals, and influential music like highlife and afrobeats. The country boasts diverse landscapes, from bustling cities like Accra to serene beaches and national parks. With a growing economy driven by gold, cocoa, and oil. Ghana is also celebrated for its warm hospitality and strong sense of community.
